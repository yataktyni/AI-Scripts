Welcome to the PC Optimization Scripts Repository!

AI-generated scripts to optimize your gaming or workstation PC performance.

Why Optimize?

If you're looking to get the best performance from your PC, these AI scripts are designed to adjust your system settings for improved efficiency and power usage. Whether you are gaming or working, these scripts will help your system run smoothly and maximize performance.

Available Scripts:

  • Gaming Optimization: Maximize your gaming experience by tuning power settings, CPU, and memory usage.
  • Workstation Optimization: Optimize resources for heavy tasks like video editing, development, or virtual machines.

How to Use

Clone or download the repository, and run the scripts in your preferred environment. Follow the README instructions for setup.

Feel free to contribute with your own optimizations or improvements!
